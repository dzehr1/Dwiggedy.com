about

A voice in the silence.

Dwiggedy moves through fashion and light like a thought you almost remember—an ethereal presence with taste, restraint, and a dry wit. At his side, Mouse and Monkey: two border collies who steal every scene without trying.

Together they are the quiet at the center of the noise—three hearts, one orbit, no rush to explain themselves.
